Welcome to my digital gallery, where each artwork is a testament to the boundless love of Jesus Christ that transcends all boundaries. As a former educator turned self-taught visual artist and Graphic Designer, my journey has been guided by a deep immersion in this love, which permeates every stroke of my brush and pixel of my design.   • Darkroom Dialogues
    Project description

    Darkroom Dialogues: Film & Photogram Series This series brings together two analog techniques—film photography and photograms—both created in the darkroom using black-and-white processes. While film captures moments through the lens, photograms use objects placed directly on photo paper to leave behind abstract, camera-less impressions. Together, they explore light, shadow, and presence in quiet conversation—one rooted in observation, the other in pure form and intuition.

    01. Film Photography - Nocturnal Solitude

    Nocturnal Solitude" is a mesmerizing black and white film photograph that captures the enchanting beauty of the night with exquisite detail and artistry. Through its evocative imagery and timeless aesthetic, the photograph invites viewers to embark on a journey of exploration and introspection, guided by the gentle glow of a solitary light source amidst the darkness.

    02. Photogram - Journey of Life

    This Photogram is an evocative mixed-media piece that combines the delicate strokes of pencil drawing with the raw reality of stepping barefoot on real pieces of glass and light bulbs. This unique composition aims to capture the essence of life's journey, juxtaposing the fragility of the human experience with the harsh realities encountered along the way.

    03. Photogram - Cosmic Prayer

    Woven throughout the fabric are spiraling threads that create intricate patterns reminiscent of cosmic spirals, symbolizing the interconnectedness of all things in the vast expanse of the universe. These spirals seem to unite the stars in a dance of celestial harmony, echoing the eternal rhythm of creation.At the center of the composition, a large, luminous circle radiates with divine light, representing the presence of God amidst the cosmos. This radiant circle serves as a focal point, drawing the viewer's gaze and inviting contemplation of the power and majesty of the divine. .

    04. Photogram - Wind

    By creating a photogram—placing these objects directly onto light-sensitive photographic paper and exposing them to light—the artist elevates the ephemeral beauty of "Wind" to new heights. Each subtle variation in light and shadow, each delicate flutter of fabric, is preserved in exquisite detail, offering viewers a window into a world suspended between reality and imagination.

    05. Photogram - Embroidery

    captivating photogram crafted with embroidered tulle and delicate, dehydrated flowers, evoking a sense of romance and nostalgia that transports viewers to a bygone era.As light filters through the layers of tulle and falls upon the dehydrated flowers, a soft, ethereal glow suffuses the scene, casting a spell of enchantment and romance. Shadows dance and flicker across the fabric, creating an atmosphere of mystery and allure that beckons viewers to linger and explore.

    06. Unveiled

    Black & White Photogram Created by folding transparent fabric directly on darkroom paper, Unveiled reveals a sculptural play of light and shadow. The fabric forms translucent tunnels and opaque curves, exploring themes of vulnerability, movement, and the quiet power of what lies just beneath the surface.

    07. Photogram - Pollination

    Photogram (Darkroom, Black & White) This camera-less image was created by placing a hand-drawn butterfly and dried flowers directly onto light-sensitive paper.
